Health Insight

Common Issues

Chronic snoring, severe daytime fatigue, unexplained seizures, and breathing difficulties can indicate underlying conditions.

The Causes

Obesity, genetics, high stress levels, neurological imbalances, and lifestyle choices are primary contributors.

Ways to Overcome

Early diagnosis via PSG/EEG is crucial. Solutions include CPAP therapy, medication, and lifestyle modifications.
